CORPORATE WELLNESS

Paradise Group joins hands with DocOnline for Employees’ Vaccination Drive

Shared By :

Dr Bhavya Kandra
Paediatrician
DocOnline

A 3.5-year-old girl came to us with itching and frequent urination for a week. Her parents wanted only an ointment to soothe the itching and were hesitant to do further tests because the child had no pain or fever.

After discussing her medical history, we learned she had two previous urinary infections last year. We recommended tests, which revealed a serious bacterial infection that required immediate treatment with IV antibiotics.

Further investigations showed that the child had a condition called Vesicoureteric Reflux (VUR), where urine flows backward from the bladder to the kidneys, causing swelling and damage. She was referred to a specialist for surgery, which will help protect her kidneys in the long run.

Impressively, 80% of our patients resolve their health issues through telemedicine alone. Thanks to early detection and treatment, we were able to prevent permanent kidney damage.

CATTLE HEALTH

DocOnline’s conducts vet camp in Chandauli for healthier cattle and stronger livelihoods

On 27 January 2026, DocOnline brought veterinary care to the farmers of Chandauli district, Uttar Pradesh. The one-day camp focused on cows and buffaloes and offered practical, hands- on services that matter to rural households and their livelihoods.

Local farmers consulted with professionals, availed immediate treatment for common ailments, and learned simple steps to keep their animals healthy throughout the year. Healthy livestock means more stable incomes, fewer emergency costs, and greater food security for families. These outcomes align closely with our aim of supporting community health beyond the clinic.

Together we can scale prevention, support rural livelihoods, and make primary animal health accessible to more communities.

WALKATHON

Stepping up together as DocOnline facilitates Hinduja Housing Finance’s Walkathon

From 1 January to 31 January 2026, over 2000 employees from Hinduja Housing Finance laced up their shoes and joined a month-long Walkathon.

The challenge turned commutes, lunch breaks, and evening strolls into moments of friendly competition and collective well-being. DocOnline’s app tracked steps in real time, while live leaderboards helped build motivation among employees.

Let’s bring more conversations around health, building enthusiasm, camaraderie, and a healthier rhythm across the workplace.

EMPLOYEE WELLNESS

DocOnline partners with Daikin to put health first with the Annual Wellness Drive

DocOnline and Daikin’s plant in Rajasthan have collaborated to deliver an on-site annual health check that saw over 1,500 employees take part. The day brought preventive care directly to the workplace, making it easy for staff to get checked without disrupting production schedules.

The camp offered core health screenings, doctor consultations, and practical health advice tailored to an industrial workforce. Employees received vital health checks, along with guidance on lifestyle, nutrition and workplace ergonomics.

DocOnline aims to provide a scalable model for reducing health-related disruptions and investing in long term workforce wellbeing. This initiative strengthens employee morale and reinforces a culture of safety, ultimately fostering a healthier and more productive workplace.

EMPLOYEE HEALTHCARE

DocOnline Facilitates Annual Health Check Camps as part of the Dr. Reddy’s Employee Health & Wellbeing Benefit Program

DocOnline conducted annual health check-ups camps in Telangana designed to support employees and the wider school staff.

In collaboration with the diagnostic partner Redcliffe Labs, the initiative provided comprehensive health screenings for all participants. The screening covered essential checks and ensured timely, quality care for every member who took part.

As we aim to support a happier, more productive workforce and a stronger local community, the camps highlight our commitment to fostering a culture of care and wellbeing.

HIGHLIGHT OF THE MONTH

A small check can change everything

Whispering Wishes Foundation, with DocOnline as its knowledge and healthcare partner, ran AI-powered breast screening and general health camps in Chiraigaon (Varanasi), Jodhpur (Rajasthan) and Gaya (Bihar).

With Smart Health AI powering breast cancer screening, the camps offered free health checkups and doctor consultations, bringing reliable screening and advice directly to women who need it most.

This initiative pushes us closer to our mission of delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare to rural women.

By combining digital innovation with on-the-ground outreach, we’re breaking barriers of distance and access, helping communities spot problems early and act sooner.
