CORPORATE WELLNESS

Paradise Group joins hands with DocOnline for Employees’ Vaccination Drive

Shared By :

Dr Bhavya Kandra
Paediatrician
DocOnline

A 3.5-year-old girl came to us with itching and frequent urination for a week. Her parents wanted only an ointment to soothe the itching and were hesitant to do further tests because the child had no pain or fever.

After discussing her medical history, we learned she had two previous urinary infections last year. We recommended tests, which revealed a serious bacterial infection that required immediate treatment with IV antibiotics.

Further investigations showed that the child had a condition called Vesicoureteric Reflux (VUR), where urine flows backward from the bladder to the kidneys, causing swelling and damage. She was referred to a specialist for surgery, which will help protect her kidneys in the long run.

Impressively, 80% of our patients resolve their health issues through telemedicine alone. Thanks to early detection and treatment, we were able to prevent permanent kidney damage.

MENTAL WELLNESS

DocOnline Hosts Two Webinars on Employees' Well-Being for Quess Corp

DocOnline conducted interactive webinars at Quess Corp for its employees on ‘Mental Wellness’ and ‘Mindful eating.’

Focusing on key aspects of physical and mental well-being, these webinars are part of DocOnline’s commitment to helping organizations like Quess Corp prioritize employee well-being.

The first webinar focused on "Mental Wellness," highlighting the importance of taking care of our mental health. The session provided simple yet effective strategies to handle stress, stay resilient, and maintain a positive outlook.

The second webinar, titled "Mindful Eating," focused on building healthy eating habits. This session aimed to help participants make better-informed food choices for a healthy mind and a healthy body.

The enthusiastic participation and positive responses from the employees show how impactful such sessions can be in promoting healthier lifestyles.
