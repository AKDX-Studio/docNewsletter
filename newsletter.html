PRACTICAL WELLNESS

HSBC Premier brings practical wellness into the spotlight with DocOnline

On February 20, HSBC hosted an exclusive fireside session for Premier customers, opening the floor to a candid and practical conversation about everyday wellbeing. The session was moderated by Nitin Mathur, Head of Customer Propositions, who steered the dialogue toward real-world challenges faced by busy professionals and families alike.

DocOnline’s experts, Dr. Alka Walavalkar and Dr. Shraddha Humnabadkar, broke down evidence-based nutrition into simple, actionable habits demonstrating that good health can fit seamlessly into even the busiest lifestyles.

The session also delved into stress management, the value of early intervention, and the importance of seeking timely support helping attendees understand how small concerns, if left unchecked, can grow into bigger issues.

Taking a truly holistic view, the conversation also explored comprehensive care solutions, including support for pet health consultations Recognising that wellbeing extends to every member of the family.

NEW PARTNERSHIPS

DocOnline Partners with Bengaluru Airport Services Limited to Take Employee Healthcare to New Heights

DocOnline and BASL collaborate to bring comprehensive digital healthcare access to all BASL employees with unlimited 24/7 consultations for employees and their families.

The partnership goes beyond routine consultations. It includes unlimited counselling sessions to support mental wellbeing, instant digital prescriptions with seamless medicine and diagnostic ordering, alongside additional benefits such as discounted OPD, dental, and pharmacy services.

At DocOnline, we believe healthcare should be accessible, proactive, and family-inclusive. Our partnership with BASL reflects a shared commitment to employee wellbeing, productivity, and long term health security.

CATTLE HEALTH

DocOnline conducts vet camp in Chandauli for healthier cattle and stronger livelihoods

On 27 January 2026, DocOnline brought veterinary care to the farmers of Chandauli district, Uttar Pradesh. The one-day camp focused on cows and buffaloes and offered practical, hands- on services that matter to rural households and their livelihoods.

Local farmers consulted with professionals, availed immediate treatment for common ailments, and learned simple steps to keep their animals healthy throughout the year. Healthy livestock means more stable incomes, fewer emergency costs, and greater food security for families. These outcomes align closely with our aim of supporting community health beyond the clinic.

Together we can scale prevention, support rural livelihoods, and make primary animal health accessible to more communities.

EMPLOYEE HEALTHCARE

DocOnline Facilitates Annual Health Check Camps as part of the Dr. Reddy’s Employee Health & Wellbeing Benefit Program

DocOnline conducted annual health check-ups camps in Telangana designed to support employees and the wider school staff.

In collaboration with the diagnostic partner Redcliffe Labs, the initiative provided comprehensive health screenings for all participants. The screening covered essential checks and ensured timely, quality care for every member who took part.

As we aim to support a happier, more productive workforce and a stronger local community, the camps highlight our commitment to fostering a culture of care and wellbeing.

WOMEN’S DAY SPECIAL

Building a Healthy Workplace Begins with Prioritizing Women’s Health

Shared By :

Sonam Rai
General Manager, Human Resources
DocOnline

On this Women’s Day, I am proud to share DocOnline’s commitment to shaping healthier ecosystems for women where every woman feels respected, supported and empowered to thrive.

We recognise that women’s health needs are distinct and deserve focused attention. Hence, we have embedded specialist teleconsultations for women’s health into our core benefits so that expert care is accessible, private, and timely.

On the ground, our community health initiatives are led by women, for women. Through rural health camps, AI-assisted cancer screenings for women, prenatal care support and livelihood programs, we are strengthening access where it is needed most.

Impressively, 80% of our patients resolve their health issues through telemedicine alone. This Women’s Day, we celebrate the possibilities of thriving workplaces and stronger communities that follow when women are cared for.

HIGHLIGHT OF THE MONTH

A small check can change everything

Whispering Wishes Foundation, with DocOnline as its knowledge and healthcare partner, ran AI-powered breast screening and general health camps in Chiraigaon (Varanasi), Jodhpur (Rajasthan) and Gaya (Bihar).

With Smart Health AI powering breast cancer screening, the camps offered free health checkups and doctor consultations, bringing reliable screening and advice directly to women who need it most.

This initiative pushes us closer to our mission of delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare to rural women.

By combining digital innovation with on-the-ground outreach, we’re breaking barriers of distance and access, helping communities spot problems early and act sooner.
