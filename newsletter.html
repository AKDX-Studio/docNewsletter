CORPORATE WELLNESS

Paradise Group joins hands with DocOnline for Employees’ Vaccination Drive

Shared By :

Dr Bhavya Kandra
Paediatrician
DocOnline

A 3.5-year-old girl came to us with itching and frequent urination for a week. Her parents wanted only an ointment to soothe the itching and were hesitant to do further tests because the child had no pain or fever.

After discussing her medical history, we learned she had two previous urinary infections last year. We recommended tests, which revealed a serious bacterial infection that required immediate treatment with IV antibiotics.

Further investigations showed that the child had a condition called Vesicoureteric Reflux (VUR), where urine flows backward from the bladder to the kidneys, causing swelling and damage. She was referred to a specialist for surgery, which will help protect her kidneys in the long run.

Impressively, 80% of our patients resolve their health issues through telemedicine alone. Thanks to early detection and treatment, we were able to prevent permanent kidney damage.

Medicine delivery now available on the DocOnline app: Comprehensive care for employees and their families

DocOnline is making everyday healthcare even more convenient. With the new Medicine Access feature, employees and their families can now order over the counter medicines, daily health essentials and regularly used medicines directly through the app. The simple, guided process also supports prescription medicines, ensuring every order remains safe, compliant and hassle free.

Getting started is quick and simple

  • Open the DocOnline app and tap Order Medicines on the homepage.
  • Enable location access and select who the order is for.
  • Search for medicines and add non-prescription items to the cart.
  • Review the order, add the delivery address and complete payment.
  • Medicines are delivered directly to the chosen address.

DocOnline takes one more step to make workplaces healthier with uninterrupted workdays, quicker access to essential medicines for employees, and a thoughtful way to extend healthcare support to their families.

COMMUNITY HEALTH

Clinic on Two Wheels Expands Its Reach to Haryana, Jharkhand, MP and Bihar to Magnify Its Impact

DocOnline expands its ‘Clinic on Two Wheels’ programme to bring essential healthcare closer to more communities.

After its launch in Rajasthan, the initiative is now being introduced in Haryana, Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh in partnership with Aadhar Housing Finance Limited, and in Bihar with IFFCO Kisan Finance Limited.

This expansion is designed to strengthen primary care access where it matters most. By placing services within reach of local populations, DocOnline aims to reduce travel barriers, enable timelier care and support local health systems.

MENTAL WELLNESS

DocOnline Hosts Two Webinars on Employees' Well-Being for Quess Corp

DocOnline conducted interactive webinars at Quess Corp for its employees on ‘Mental Wellness’ and ‘Mindful eating.’

Focusing on key aspects of physical and mental well-being, these webinars are part of DocOnline’s commitment to helping organizations like Quess Corp prioritize employee well-being.

The first webinar focused on "Mental Wellness," highlighting the importance of taking care of our mental health. The session provided simple yet effective strategies to handle stress, stay resilient, and maintain a positive outlook.

The second webinar, titled "Mindful Eating," focused on building healthy eating habits. This session aimed to help participants make better-informed food choices for a healthy mind and a healthy body.

The enthusiastic participation and positive responses from the employees show how impactful such sessions can be in promoting healthier lifestyles.

HIGHLIGHT OF THE MONTH

A small check can change everything

Whispering Wishes Foundation, with DocOnline as its knowledge and healthcare partner, ran AI-powered breast screening and general health camps in Chiraigaon (Varanasi), Jodhpur (Rajasthan) and Gaya (Bihar).

With Smart Health AI powering breast cancer screening, the camps offered free health checkups and doctor consultations, bringing reliable screening and advice directly to women who need it most.

This initiative pushes us closer to our mission of delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare to rural women.

By combining digital innovation with on-the-ground outreach, we’re breaking barriers of distance and access, helping communities spot problems early and act sooner.
