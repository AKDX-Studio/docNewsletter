Great Place to Work

DocOnline Recognised as a Great Place to Work for the Fourth Consecutive Year

DocOnline has once again been certified as a Great Place to Work, marking its fourth consecutive year of recognition.

This certification reflects the culture we’ve collectively shaped with mutual respect, trust, and collaboration. At DocOnline, we look forward to creating a work environment where everybody feels supported, valued, and empowered to do their best work.

At the heart of this achievement lies our commitment to the vision of enabling healthier families, workplaces, and communities.

Workplace Wellness

ONGC Petro additions Limited (OPaL) X DocOnline towards Effortless Employee Wellbeing Everyday

On 15 April 2026, OPaL conducted awareness sessions across its Vadodara and Dahej locations to introduce the initiative, setting the tone for a people-first approach to health.

This collaboration offers comprehensive digital healthcare and mental wellness support to employees and their families with 24x7 unlimited doctor consultations across 25+ specialties, personalized diet plan and mental wellness support through EAP with confidential counselling. The services also include veterinary consultations for pets.

With on-demand healthcare access to its workforce, we continue to invest in building a resilient, engaged, and future-ready organisation.

Community Health

Another Step towards Bringing Healthcare Closer to Communities with Outreach in Chandauli

During mid-April, DocOnline partnered with NGO partner Whispering Wishes Foundation to step up towards community health with IRDHS, Integrated Rural Digi-Physical Healthcare Services that combine on-ground presence with digital access.

The impact unfolded in three layers. The initiative, Clinic on 2-Wheels, reached communities at their doorstep on April 17. On April 18, digital consultations connected patients to doctors in real time, and April 20, health camps promoted awareness and encouraged preventive care.

It was a 360-degree approach to make care more accessible, faster, and smarter for the locals.

Highlight of the Month

DocOnline Featured in ‘Earth Stories’ for its Ground Impact now in the Global Spotlight

The ‘Clinic on 2-Wheels’ initiative, designed to bridge the gap between underserved rural communities and expert medical care, has been recognised in the Swedish Chamber of Commerce India’s ‘Earth Stories.’

The platform, ‘Earth Stories’ showcases impactful initiatives by Swedish companies driving change across health, innovation, and sustainability.

By taking healthcare directly to the doorstep, ‘Clinic on 2-Wheels’ enables timely access, early intervention, and stronger health awareness on the ground.

With a vision to reach 10,000 villages by 2027, DocOnline continues to scale its CSR efforts to drive meaningful, last-mile health transformation.

WOMEN’S DAY SPECIAL

Building a Healthy Workplace Begins with Prioritizing Women’s Health

Shared By :

Sonam Rai
General Manager, Human Resources
DocOnline

On this Women’s Day, I am proud to share DocOnline’s commitment to shaping healthier ecosystem for women where every woman feels respected, supported and empowered to thrive.

At DocOnline, gender diversity is central to who we are. Women form half of our workforce, contributing their expertise across teams. This commitment also extends to the care we provide.

We offer 24/7 medical support on the DocOnline app, enabling access to specialist doctors for women’s health. Our medical network reflects this commitment with 70% of doctors on the DocOnline platform being women.

Among DocOnline employees, over 60% of consultations on the app are sought by our women employees, taking charge of their health with dignity, and timely expert care.

On the ground, our community health initiatives are led by women, for women. Through rural health camps, AI-assisted cancer screenings for women, prenatal care support and employment opportunities, we are strengthening access where it is needed most.

Impressively, 80% of our patients resolve their health issues through telemedicine alone. This Women’s Day, we celebrate the healthier workplaces and stronger communities that follow when women’s health and wellbeing are prioritized.

HIGHLIGHT OF THE MONTH

A small check can change everything

Whispering Wishes Foundation, with DocOnline as its knowledge and healthcare partner, ran AI-powered breast screening and general health camps in Chiraigaon (Varanasi), Jodhpur (Rajasthan) and Gaya (Bihar).

With Smart Health AI powering breast cancer screening, the camps offered free health checkups and doctor consultations, bringing reliable screening and advice directly to women who need it most.

This initiative pushes us closer to our mission of delivering compassionate, high-quality healthcare to rural women.

By combining digital innovation with on-the-ground outreach, we’re breaking barriers of distance and access, helping communities spot problems early and act sooner.
